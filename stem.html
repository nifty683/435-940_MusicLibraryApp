Instructions

Below are 4 songs that have been separated into stems. These stems can be volume controlled individually. Here are a few things to keep in mind while using the stem player.

  • Only use the play and pause button on the vocal track.
  • Seeking through a track will result in the timing of the song being not synchronized.
    • In order to fix this synchronization error. Set all the stems to time 0:00. Or refresh the page.
